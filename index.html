Nike Air Jordan 1 Lost and Found

The Jordan Brand team responsible for developing the AJ1 Chicago obsessed about every detail of the shoe's design to bring this nostalgic story to life. Jordan designers studied multiple pairs of original AJ1s in varying states of distress to capture the nuances of the shoe's look, described as having "light-to-no wear but with materials that sat for years in a box and didn't withstand the test of time".
